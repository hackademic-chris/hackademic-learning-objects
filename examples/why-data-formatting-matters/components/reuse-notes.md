# Reuse notes

## Free use

Use as a LinkedIn post, newsletter article, or short public Skool lesson.

Best free hook:

**Most AI adoption problems are not AI problems. Some are data formatting problems wearing a nicer jacket.**

The free version should include:

- The messy customer example
- The structured JSON comparison
- Three ways formatting affects AI and automation
- One short manager-level reflection question

- AI readiness
- Workflow automation
- Data governance
- CRM cleanup
- Reporting modernization
